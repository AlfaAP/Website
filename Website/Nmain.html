📖 Welcome to Script by Public!

Imagine a world where stories aren’t just written by one person—but by all of us together! 🌍✨

At @scriptbypublic.in, we’re building something unique: a community-driven script where **your** ideas shape the storyline. Here’s how it works:

  • 📜 An initial story is shared to kick things off.
  • 🗳️ Every week, a Google Form is launched for you to submit your ideas.
  • 🎭 The best contributions are woven into the script!
  • 🏆 Contributors get a **shoutout** every weekend, along with a story progress summary.

So, what are you waiting for? Join us in crafting a story that belongs to **everyone**! 🚀🎬
